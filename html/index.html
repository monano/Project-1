>How To Play<

Instructions

  • Each player has three pieces. and there is two colors: black and white.
  • The board is empty to begin the game, and players take turns placing their pieces on the empty gameboard.
  • Once all pieces are placed (no player allowed to make a mill in the placing step), play proceeds with each player moving one of their pieces per turn.
  • A piece may move to any adjacent point on the board, diagonally move is not allowed.
  • The winner is the first player to align their three pieces on a vertical line or horizontal line.
